Meet the Author

Karimot Isiaka is like a book with many chapters. She currently finds meaning in living a life that first, inspires her and then inspires other people to stay positive and develop a brighter perspective about life.
She is a certified life coach, who helps people who struggle with low self esteem and self-doubt develop healthy self-esteem and self-confidence. Karimot has helped hundreds of people build self-confidence and trained over 2,000 people on various personal development subjects. She loves listening to beautiful music, she loves learning new things and she is a sucker for progress. She believes in Allah. Allah is her greatest source.
