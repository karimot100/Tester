About the Book

Confidence Within is a book that teaches us that Confidence is first an inside job. It teaches us how to build healthy confidence in ourselves and develop a healthy self-esteem. The author, Karimot Isiaka showed us how she bounced back after so many years of living with self-doubt and lack of confidence. She shared some of her experiences like; how she moved from not going a radio station to claim her gift because she was afraid, to co-hosting two radio shows for about a year. She also shared about how she moved from not being bold enough to speak in from of her classmates to speaking and training at various human capital development programs (both online and offline).
